About Me

My name is Chao Liu and I am a student at Zhejiang University in Hangzhou, Zhejiang. My research interests include computer vision, curvilinear structure reconstruction, and medical image processing.

My Work

Here are some of my recent research projects:

  • Computer Vision

    Research on computer vision including object detection, segmentation, and tracking, and deep learning-based approaches for image and video analysis.

  • Curvilinear Structure Reconstruction

    Developed deep learning-based approaches for reconstructing curvilinear structures from medical images, and worked on developing an automatic segmentation algorithm for brain tumors in MRI scans.

  • Medical Image Processing

    Research on medical image processing including image registration, image fusion, and image denoising, with a focus on improving the accuracy and efficiency of medical diagnosis.
